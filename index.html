Renny Pereira's mini resume

Letrux Music Video

One of 11 artists commissioned to animate the music video for the song "Abalos Sísmicos" from Brazilian indie artist Letrux. The song is part of the Latin Grammy nominated album "Letrux aos Prantos".

Leguminhos

Art director, character designer, illustrator and storyboard artist for pre-school animated musical webseries about vegetables friends. Responsible for creating the character designs, color schemes and art direction of the pilot episode and all episodes for season 1.

Quidd

Illustrated and designed a variety of different digital goods such as stickers and trading cards for a number of licenses (such as Bob’s Burgers, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Game of Thrones, Adventure Time, Star Trek, Marvel, Rick and Morty, among others).
